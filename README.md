# Tata-Data-Visualisation-Project
Power BI dashboard and business insights for Tata's global sales data.

## What I Did:
- *Data Cleaning (ETL):* Transformed the dataset in Power BI by filtering the Quantity and UnitPrice columns to be *greater than 0* to remove errors.
- *Data Modeling:* Created a calculated column for Revenue using the formula: Quantity × Unit Price.
- *Visualizations Created:*
  - *Line Chart:* Monthly Revenue Trend for 2011 (showed high demand during the holiday season).
  - *Clustered Bar Charts:* Top 10 Countries by Revenue and Revenue by CustomerID (Excluded UK as it dominates the sales).
  - *Treemap:* Used as a smart alternative to maps to show global product demand clearly in rectangle shapes.

## Tools Used:
- Power BI Desktop
